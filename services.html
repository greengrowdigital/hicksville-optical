FAQ

Quick answers.

Do you accept walk-ins?+
Yes — when the schedule allows. Call ahead (516) 931-1010 and we'll tell you the wait.
Which insurance plans do you take?+
VSP, EyeMed, Davis Vision, Spectera, Aetna, and most major medical carriers.
How long does an eye exam take?+
New patients: 60 minutes. Annual checks: 30 minutes. Contact lens fittings: 45 minutes.
Can I buy frames without an exam?+
Absolutely. Bring a valid prescription (under 2 years old) and your pupillary distance.
Do you sell contact lenses online?+
We can ship your annual supply directly. Reorder by phone, email, or text.
