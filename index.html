03 · The Practice

A neighborhood practice.
Built over decades.

Hicksville Optical opened its doors on Newbridge Road with a simple idea: take the time. Don't rush the exam, don't push the upgrade, don't pretend the catalogue knows better than the patient in front of you.

Two decades later, the practice still runs the same way. Same chair. Same patience. Same insistence that every prescription be checked, every fitting be honest, and every frame leave the bench finished by hand.
